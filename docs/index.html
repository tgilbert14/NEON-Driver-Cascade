Each app is a deep, honest explorer for one NEON data product — pick one and dive in. They share a design, a statistics-show-their-work ethic, and the same field sites, so the Driver Cascade can tie them together.

About the suite

Built by Desert Data Labs on real data from the National Ecological Observatory Network (NEON, operated by Battelle, funded by NSF). Every app loads pre-bundled snapshots so there are no live-API waits, refreshes itself on a monthly off-peak schedule, and leads with transparent, well-sourced methods — detection-corrected abundance, honest diversity estimators, area-corrected detectability, interval-censored phenology, and data-quality flags you can click and download. Educational data-exploration tools, not affiliated with NEON, Battelle, or the NSF.
